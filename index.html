Serving with Heart,
Leading with Purpose

The Lions Club of Eheliyagoda Legacy is a proud member of Lions Clubs International, operating under District 306 D7 in Sri Lanka.

Guided by our theme "Our Service Today, Our Legacy Tomorrow," we have been actively working to uplift the lives of people in Eheliyagoda and surrounding communities through health initiatives, educational programs, environmental conservation, and disaster relief efforts.

A Legacy in the Making

From our charter to the road ahead — the journey of a club that's just getting started.

June 2026

Club Chartered

Lions Club of Eheliyagoda Legacy was officially chartered under Lions Clubs International, District 306 D7, beginning our mission to serve the community.

2026

A Founding Team of 42

We came together as 42 dedicated founding members and elected our first board of directors, ready to serve Eheliyagoda and beyond.

2026

Our Online Home

Launched our official website to share our story, connect with the community, and welcome new members to the Lions family.

The Road Ahead

Our First Service Projects

Preparing to launch our first initiatives — a blood donation camp, scholarship awards, and an environmental drive across the region.

Building a Lasting Legacy
